Utilty

Dior Sauvage Eau de Parfum is a men's fragrance inspired by the desert at sunset. For Sauvage Eau de Parfum, the House of Dior has chosen an extraordinary vanilla from Papua New Guinea. It imparts a luscious exotic quality over a woody, amber base, giving Sauvage a sensual accent. For a moment it was colored a deep blue, when the coolness of the night mixed with the heat of the desert.
